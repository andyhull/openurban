Help build OpenUrban! - 150x100x1 Challenge
 
November 30th – December 31st, 2012
OpenUrban needs your help to fill out its database! So we're asking friends and supporters to help us create 150 new project pages of 100 words or more within 1 month – the 150X100X1 Challenge. You can create a page about any urban development of any size anywhere in the world. If you're not sure where to start we recommend making a page about something in your city or community. Is there a new transit station or apartment building planned for your neighborhood?


Did we mention prizes?!
  • Don't Wait Award(s)  –  the first 5 individuals to make pages after November 30th will each receive a limited edition hand printed poster.
  • Best Research Award  –  the best written and researched page will receive an OpenUrban t-shirt and a packet of street stickers. This prize applies to new edits of existing pages as well.
  • Most Prolific Award  –  the person with the most pages (100 words/page) will receive a t-shirt, stickers, a hand printed poster, and our eternal gratitude.
 
 
Who we are:
OpenUrban was founded by Reed Duecy-Gibbs, Jesse Honsa, Irene Guzmán, and Ali Onat Turker. We are a group of architects, designers, and urbanists living and working worldwide.
